Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is known as the "City of Light". It is famous for its art, culture, and stunning architecture. Visitors can enjoy historical landmarks, world-class museums, and delightful cuisine.

  1. Eiffel Tower – Iconic landmark offering breathtaking views of the city.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to thousands of masterpieces including the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – Famous Gothic cathedral with magnificent architecture.
  4. Sacré-Cœur Basilica – Beautiful basilica on Montmartre hill with panoramic views.
  5. Champs-Élysées – A world-renowned avenue for shopping and entertainment.
  • Croissants – Flaky, buttery pastries perfect for breakfast.
  • Macarons – Colorful and sweet almond-based treats.
  • Escargot – Traditional French snails cooked in garlic and butter.
  • Baguette – Classic French bread with a crispy crust.
  • Crème Brûlée – Rich dessert with a caramelized sugar topping.
